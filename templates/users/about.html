About The Lair

Welcome to Pizza Lair where everyone is welcome! We are a family run business who are passionate about creating a welcoming atmosphere where all dietary preferences and restrictions are accommodated. At our pizza place, we offer a variety of options for everyone, including those who follow a vegan, gluten-free, or organic diet. Our pizzas are made from scratch using only the freshest, highest quality ingredients.

We believe that pizza should be enjoyed by everyone, regardless of dietary restrictions. That's why we have taken great care to create a menu that caters to all tastes and preferences. Whether you are looking for a classic margherita pizza or a vegan option with plant-based cheese, we've got you covered. We also understand the importance of sustainability and environmental responsibility, which is why we use eco-friendly packaging and strive to source our ingredients locally whenever possible.
